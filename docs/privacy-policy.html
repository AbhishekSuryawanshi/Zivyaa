Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2026

At ZIVYAA, your privacy is sacred. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We are committed to transparency and protecting your personal data.

01

Information We Collect

To provide personalized astrological insights, we collect:

  • Profile Information: Name, date of birth, time of birth, and place of birth
  • App Preferences: Language selection, notification settings, and display preferences
  • Usage Data: App interaction patterns, feature usage, and session duration
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version

We do not collect passwords, financial information, or government-issued IDs.

02

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Generate your personalized Kundali (birth chart) and astrological calculations
  • Provide daily guidance, cosmic insights, and timing recommendations
  • Enable profile management for family members
  • Send notifications you have opted into (daily guidance, cosmic alerts)
  • Improve app performance and fix technical issues
03

Data Storage & Security

Your profile and astrological data is stored locally on your device using secure app storage. All astrological calculations are performed on-device using our Swiss Ephemeris engine.

We use Firebase (Google Cloud) for remote configuration and analytics. Data transmitted to our servers is encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS encryption.

04

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data.

We may share limited data only with:

  • Firebase (Google): For app analytics and remote configuration
  • Apple: For App Store services, crash reporting, and subscription management
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process

We never share your birth details, chart data, or personal insights with any third party.

05

Your Rights & Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Edit: Update your profile information at any time
  • Delete: Remove your profile and all associated data from the app
  • Notifications: Enable or disable notifications from Settings
  • Data Export: Generate PDF reports of your astrological data
  • Account Deletion: Request complete account deletion via Settings or by contacting us
06

Notifications

ZIVYAA sends notifications only if you explicitly opt in. You can expect:

  • Maximum 1-2 notifications per day
  • Daily guidance with personalized cosmic insights
  • Upcoming cosmic alerts (transits, favorable periods)

No fear-based, urgent, or anxiety-inducing messages. You can disable notifications at any time from Settings.

07

Third-Party Services

ZIVYAA integrates with the following services:

  • Firebase Firestore: Remote configuration and feature flags
  • Apple StoreKit: In-app subscription management
  • Swiss Ephemeris: On-device astronomical calculation library

Each service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

08

Children's Privacy

ZIVYAA is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of ZIVYAA after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10

Spiritual Disclaimer

ZIVYAA provides insights rooted in Vedic traditions for spiritual exploration and self-reflection. The app is not a substitute for professional advice in health, financial, or legal matters. All astrological content is generated algorithmically and should be treated as guidance, not prediction.
